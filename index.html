The Appealing and Thriving Community of Waray

The Waray people are an ethnolinguistic group from the Philippines who lives in the Eastern Visayas, a region from the Philippines. They are a large indigenous group, possessing a wide range of cultural practices, distinct names, and a big population. The term ”Waray-waray“ is an umbrella reference surrounding the Waray people and their language.
