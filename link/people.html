The Music Man of Samar, Jose Cinco Gomez

Jose Cinco Gomez was born in Brgy. Tabok, Calbayog City on February 27, 1911. He could play all kinds of instruments at the age of 7, ranging from Saxophone, Trumpet, Clarinet, etc. He had the great privilege of playing at the Malaca√±ang Place reception. In that special event, he composed the “Moonlight Cruise in Casiana” to honor President Manuel Roxas on his prestige and beautiful presidential yacht named after him, “Casiana”.

Pablo Rebadulla continued the tradition of Folk Poetry in his composition “Basuni”. The revolutionary love song may have evolved from the folk songs of Leyte and Samar such as Folk songs, Sugbo notes, making the bulk of documented folk poetry in Waray. These songs were sang to “woo the maidens since most of them dwell in love.”
